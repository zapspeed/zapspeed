Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy terms of the mobile application Zapspeed (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), developed and published by World of Curiosity (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer", "We", or "Us"), located in the United States.

1. What Data We Collect and How We Use It

The Application processes only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide its core features and verify your upfront purchase. All personal and activity data is stored locally on your device.

  • Location Data (GPS):
    • Purpose: To calculate your cycling speed, distance, and current trip stats in real-time.
    • Legal Basis: Explicit user consent granted before enabling the GPS feature.
  • Ride History and Statistics (Training Data):
    • Purpose: To log and display your past cycling activities, including: total distance, duration, average speed, and maximum speed.
    • Data Storage: All ride history is stored exclusively on your local device storage. We do not upload your training data, routes, or statistics to any external servers.
  • Network and IP Data (Weather Info):
    • Purpose: To fetch real-time weather updates from third-party API providers based on your device's approximate or precise location.
  • Device Storage Access (Audio Player):
    • Purpose: To allow you to play your local audio files and manage your playlists. The Application does not upload your local music files or playlist configurations to any remote servers.

2. Payment Processing and Upfront Purchase

The Application is a paid product available for a one-time purchase fee via official app stores.

  • No Financial Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process your credit card numbers, bank account details, or billing addresses.
  • Third-Party Processors: All payments are handled securely by the app store platforms (Google Play Store / Apple App Store).
  • Transaction Tokens: The Application only receives an anonymized transaction token or receipt from the respective app store to verify your purchase status and unlock access to the download.

3. Custom URLs and JSON Services (Audio Player)

The Application allows users to manually add custom URLs for external audio streams and configuration JSON files.

The Developer does not monitor, store, or assume responsibility for any audio content played via external URLs provided by the user. These connections are made directly between your device and the target server hosting the URL.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

  1. Weather API Providers: To retrieve weather forecasts, your geographical coordinates are sent to the external weather API. This data is typically anonymized by the provider.
  2. App Store Platforms: Transaction metadata is processed by Google or Apple strictly to manage your download and licensing.
  3. Legal Compliance: We may disclose data if required to do so by applicable US federal or state laws.

5. US State Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA / VCDPA)

If you are a resident of California, Virginia, or other US states with active privacy regulations, the following provisions apply:

  • No Sale or Sharing of Personal Information: We do not sell your personal information, nor do we share it with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Collection of Sensitive Personal Information: Our collection of precise geolocation data is strictly limited to the local functionality of the speedometer and ride tracker on your device. We do not transmit or store this data remotely.
  • Right to Know and Delete: Since all your training data and history are stored locally, you can exercise your right to access or delete this information directly by managing or deleting the app data on your device.

6. Device Permissions

To function correctly, the Application requires the user to grant the following system permissions:

  • Location: Required for the speedometer, recording ride history, and fetching weather data.
  • Storage / Media Access: Required to read and play local music files.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch weather data, load external audio URL streams, parse JSON playlists, and verify your app store license.

You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device's system settings. However, doing so may disable certain features of the Application.

7. User Rights and Data Control (GDPR/International)

In accordance with international privacy laws, you retain full control over your data:

  • Since your ride history, stats, and playlists are stored locally, you can permanently delete all your data at any time. You can do this by clearing the Application's cache/data in your device's system settings or by uninstalling the Application.
  • Your purchase history remains tied to your Google or Apple account and is subject to their respective privacy policies.
